Spotlight on Playgroup

"Learning Through Play: Our Playgroup students explore the world through interactive games, creative storytelling, and joyful discovery—building a foundation of curiosity and confidence."

In our Playgroup, we focus on nurturing social skills and emotional development in a safe, colorful environment. Through hands-on activities and nationally-recognised AFAQ materials, we make every school day an adventure of learning.
